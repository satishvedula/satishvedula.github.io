Welcome to my Homepage!

Hi! I am Satish. I am currently pursuing my PhD in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the FAMU-FSU College of Engineering, Tallahassee, Florida, USA. I have earned the M.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from The University of Alabama in Huntsville, Huntsville, AL, USA in 2019 and a B.Tech. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from VIT University, Chennai, India in 2017. My research interests lie in the intersection of Optimization, Control and Modeling.

Currently, my research is focussed on investigating the battery degradation in hybrid energy systems such as Hybrid Electric vehicles, Shipboard Power Systems etc. The goal is to study a battery degradation heuristic measure which caputures the degradation effectively. Another research topic revolves around the use of fault resilient optimization methods for Inverter-Based Resources.
